About Cynthia Marie

Hi! My name is Cynthia Marie Rivera Sánchez and I am a junior undergraduate student at University of Puerto Rico - Río Piedras Campus. My major is in Computational Mathematics and Statistics; I am also seeking a second major in Computer Science and a minor in Human Rights. My interests in the discipline are algebra and statistics. Additionally, I really like programming and learning new languages.
